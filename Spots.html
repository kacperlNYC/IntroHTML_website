My hangout spots

When I'm not at school, I love to go to these places.

Forest Park

The closest park to me. Amazing for:

  • Peaceful Walks through the trees.
  • Energetic Bike Rides.

Dekalb Market Hall

With it being so close to the school, it is a great place to chill with friends and grab a bite.

Rockaway Beach

The best place to go to cooldown on a hot day. There is nothing better then relaxing in the sand or playing around in the water.
